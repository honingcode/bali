Experience the wonders of Bali ✳︎

  • BEST TIME TO VISIT

    The climate in Bali is tropical year-round, so there is no bad time to visit. The dry, coolest period runs from April to October, while the wet, hottest period runs from November to March.

    MAIN ATTRACTIONS

    There are many attractions in Bali, including the temples of Pura Tanah Lot and Pura Besakih, the beaches of Kuta, Seminyak, and Nusa Dua, and the rice terraces of Tegalalang and Jatiluwih.

    POPULAR ACTIVITIES

    Popular activities in Bali include visiting temples, diving, snorkeling, surfing, yoga, rice terrace tours, and visiting local markets and villages..
